Burger Time! is a cooking simulation/game written in C# with Unity.

About The Game

Burger Time! is a cooking simulation/game that consists of making as many burgers as you can under a min. The only controls needed is your mouse. The ingredients are located on the left side of the player. On the right, there are sample burgers. In front of the player there is a red square and that's where the burger that the player is making will appear. The video shown above does not include the audio of the game because of some issues I had with the recording software. The song I used for the Main Menu music is "Bubblegum K.K" which is a popular song from the game "Animal Crossing". Also, when playing the game, whenever the burger patties hit the stove, there is a sizzling sound that plays.
